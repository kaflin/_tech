How It Started

From a Dream to Nepal's #1 QA Lab

TechWorld Lab was founded in 2019 by a group of senior QA engineers who noticed a massive gap: talented Nepali graduates couldn't find jobs in tech because institutions weren't teaching real-world automation skills.

We started with 12 students in a single room. Today we have trained over 1,200 engineers, placed them in companies like Leapfrog, Cotiviti, CloudFactory, Fusemachines, and top firms abroad.
